Event summary

Volcanic eruption Popocatepetl is expected to have a low humanitarian impact based on the magnitude and the affected population and their vulnerability.

Name: Popocatepetl
Lat/Lon: 19.023 , -98.622
Event Date: 28 Feb 2024 07:23 UTC
