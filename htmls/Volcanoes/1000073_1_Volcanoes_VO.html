Event summary

Volcanic eruption Ubinas is expected to have a low humanitarian impact based on the magnitude and the affected population and their vulnerability.

Name: Ubinas
Lat/Lon: -16.355 , -70.903
Event Date: 03 Jul 2023 11:37 UTC
Exposed Population 30km: About 15000 people within 30km
Exposed Population 100km: About 1120000 people within 100km
Max Volc. Explosivity Index VEI: 5
