Event summary

Volcanic eruption Lewotobi is expected to have a low humanitarian impact based on the magnitude and the affected population and their vulnerability.

The eruption happened in Indonesia , Province of Nusa Tenggara Timur (population 3,515,623) . The nearest places are Nobo, Lewotobi, Tabana, Kedang, Wairunu, Kloreamang, Kringa, Lamawolo, Liko, Werangdetoeng, and Nita.
