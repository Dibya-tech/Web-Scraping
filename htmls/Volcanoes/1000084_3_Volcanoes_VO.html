Event summary

Volcanic eruption Marapi is expected to have a low humanitarian impact based on the magnitude and the affected population and their vulnerability.

Exposed population

The eruption happened in Indonesia , Province of Sumatera Barat (population 4,301,938) . The nearest places are Kotabaroe, Bulakan, Bukittinggi, Batusangkar, Gadoet, Batutabal, Malalo, Bantjah, PajaKumbuh, Tarok, and Muaro Paiti.
