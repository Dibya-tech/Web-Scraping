Event summary

Volcanic eruption Krakatau is expected to have a low humanitarian impact based on the magnitude and the affected population and their vulnerability.

Exposed population

The eruption happened in Indonesia , Province of Lampung (population 6,459,217) . The nearest places are Kalianda, Bakau, Gajam, Kiluan, Kolitjaah, Anjer-Kidu, Umbulan Pepanggar, Kekatang, Bantarwaru, Labuhan, and Marbu.
