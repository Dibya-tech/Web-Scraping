Event summary

Volcanic eruption Semeru is expected to have a low humanitarian impact based on the magnitude and the affected population and their vulnerability.

Exposed population

The eruption happened in Indonesia , Province of Jawa Timur (population 32,960,710) . The nearest places are Gunungsriti, Gunungsawar, Poncokusumo, Gubukklakah, Bambang, Pasirian, Sendoera, Danuredjo, Toempang, Wadjak, and Surabaja.
