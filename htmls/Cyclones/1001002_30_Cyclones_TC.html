          StormSurge

          1.5 m

          The maximum Storm surge height is  1.5m in Gongkou, China. This height is estimated for 04 Sep 2023 21:00 UTC .

          The JRC has developed an experimental global storm surge model. The calculations are published about 20 minutes after a new advisory is detected by GDACS. The calculations identify the populated places affected by storm surge up to three days in advance, using the forecasted track. When forecasts change, the associated storm surge changes too and alert levels may go up or down. All links, data, statistics and maps refer to the latest available calculation. If the calculation for the last advisory is not completed, the latest available calculation is shown.

          Technical Issue affecting Storm Surge Calculations

          We are encountering a technical issue affecting the accuracy of Storm Surge calculations derived from the JTWC (Joint Typhoon Warning Centre). Therefore we recommend to refer to the calculations based on the GFS (Global Forecast System) until the technical issue is solved. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
